17 /**
18  * RefExp statements map uses of expressions back to their definitions.
19  * RefExp is a subclass of Unary, holding an ordinary Exp pointer,
20  * and a pointer to a defining statement (could be a phi assignment).
21  * This is used for subscripting SSA variables. Example:
22  * m[1000]
23  * becomes
24  * m[1000]{3}
25  * if defined at statement 3.
26  *
27  * The integer is really a pointer to the defining statement,
28  * printed as the statement number for compactness.
29  * If the expression is not explicitly defined anywhere,
30  * the defining statement is nullptr.
31  */
