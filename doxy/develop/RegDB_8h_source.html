89  /// Process the effects of overlapped registers for \p stmt.
90  /// Example: (x86 register overlap)
91  /// Suppose \p stmt is %ax := 0x1234, and %eax and %ah are used in the procedure that contains
92  /// \p stmt. Then we need to generate two additional statements:
93  /// *32* %eax := (%eax & 0xFFFF0000) | %ax, and
94  /// *8* %ah := %ax@[0..7]
95  /// to model the effects of overlapped registers. This procedure returns the additional
