127  /**
128  * The progression of return information is as follows:
129  * First, reaching definitions are collected in the DefCollector col. These are not sorted or
130  * filtered. Second, some of those definitions make it to the modifieds list, which is sorted
131  * and filtered. These are the locations that are modified by the enclosing procedure. As
132  * locations are proved to be preserved (with NO modification, not even sp = sp+4), they are
133  * removed from this list. Defines in calls to the enclosing procedure are based on this list.
134  * Third, the modifications are initially copied to the returns list (also sorted and filtered,
135  * but the returns have RHS where the modifieds don't). Locations not live at any caller are
136  * removed from the returns, but not from the modifieds.
137  */
138  DefCollector m_col;
139 
140  /// A list of assignments that represents the locations modified by the enclosing procedure.
